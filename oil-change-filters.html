Oil Change & Filters

We offer both conventional and synthetic oil changes, always paired with a genuine filter replacement.

  • Oil drain and refill with high quality engine oil (brand of your choice)

  • Genuine oil filter replacement

  • Air filter and cabin filter inspection (replacement if needed)

  • Multi point fluid top up (coolant, brake fluid, washer fluid)

  • Complimentary vehicle health check

Quality Full Work

We use only manufacturer recommended oil grades and filters to maintain your engine’s warranty and longevity.

100% Work Satisfication

If you’re not happy with the service, we’ll re do it at no charge. Plus, we offer a free top up between services.

1. What’s included in a full car service?

A full service includes oil change, filter replacements, brake inspection, fluid top ups, and a detailed health report.

2. How long does a standard service take?

An oil change typically takes 30–45 minutes. A full service may take 2–3 hours.

3. Is your service covered by warranty?

Yes, we provide a warranty on all parts and labour. Our invoices are accepted by all major insurers for warranty claims.

4. How often should I change my oil?

For conventional oil, every 5,000 km or 6 months. For synthetic, up to 10,000 km or 12 months.

5. Do you use genuine oil filters?

Absolutely. We always use OEM or high quality aftermarket filters that meet manufacturer specifications.
