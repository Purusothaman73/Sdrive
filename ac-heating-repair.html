AC & Heating Repair

Stay cool in Chennai’s heat—and warm when needed.

Our AC specialists restore your car’s climate control to factory specifications using state of the art equipment.

  • • AC system performance check with pressure gauges

  • • Refrigerant recovery, vacuum test, and precise recharge

  • • Compressor, condenser, and evaporator inspection

  • • Cabin air filter replacement

  • • Heater core flush and blower motor check

Quality Full Work

We use original or high quality aftermarket AC components and environmentally safe refrigerants.

100% Work Satisfication

If your AC doesn’t blow as cold as expected within the first week, we’ll re inspect and fix it free of charge.

1. How often should the AC be serviced?

We recommend an AC health check once a year, ideally before summer.

2. Do you provide emergency roadside assistance?

Yes, we offer 24/7 roadside assistance for AC breakdowns and other issues.

3. Is your service covered by warranty?

All AC repairs include a 6 month warranty on parts and labour.

4. Why does my AC smell bad?

Usually due to mold or bacteria in the evaporator or a dirty cabin air filter. We offer cleaning and filter replacement to eliminate odours.

5. Can you fix AC leaks?

Yes, we use UV dye and electronic leak detectors to locate and repair leaks before recharging the system.
